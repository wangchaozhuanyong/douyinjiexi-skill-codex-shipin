Use this when writing captions, voiceover, production notes, or risk checks.

## Risk Categories To Avoid

- Low originality: copying frames, subtitles, voice, music, exact wording, shot order, or simple crop/mirror/BGM changes.
- Low quality: black/white screen, single-image slideshow with heavy text, unclear topic, template-heavy filler.
- External diversion: phone number, WeChat, QQ group, QR code, third-party purchase path, private-domain wording.
- Absolute or exaggerated claims: best, first, only, guaranteed, 100%, instant results, final proof without evidence.
- Clickbait or manipulation: shock, regret bait, forced like/follow/comment, incomplete bait.
- Illegal, dangerous, vulgar, adult, gambling, scam, political/military sensitive, medical/finance claims without qualification.
- False authority: official endorsement, state/TV/expert/doctor recommendation without proof.

## Allowed Reference Scope

Allowed:

- topic
- audience intent
- hook pattern
- pacing
- scene count
- information hierarchy
- broad mood or genre

Not allowed:

- original media frames
- source subtitles
- source voice or music
- source text with minor edits
- source person likeness or identity
- platform UI/watermark/logo recreation

## Copywriting Defaults

- Keep captions short and clear.
- Include 2-5 relevant hashtags.
- Avoid URLs and contact details.
- Use "参考", "思路", "适合", "可以", "建议结合实际情况" instead of guaranteed claims.
- State that platform review is not guaranteed in production notes, not in the public caption unless useful.
