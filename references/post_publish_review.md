# Post-Publish Review

Use this only after a video was published with explicit user authorization.

## Review Signals

- Views.
- 2-second hold rate when visible.
- 5-second hold rate when visible.
- Average watch duration.
- Completion rate.
- Likes, comments, saves, shares.
- User confusion in comments.
- Cover warnings or quality suggestions.

## Learning Loop

Record what changed for the next video:

- Topic type.
- Hook style.
- First-five-second visual.
- Script rhythm.
- Evidence ratio.
- Cover direction.
- Caption length.
- Hashtags.

Do not store cookies, passwords, SMS codes, or private account secrets.
